# Council Skill

Multi-Agent Debate System für strukturierte Entscheidungsfindung.

## Modi

| Modus | Rounds | Agenten | Für |
|-------|--------|---------|-----|
| QUICK | 1 | 4 parallel | Schnelle Validierung |
| EXPERT | 2 | 3-4 Domain-Experts | Fachfragen |
| DEBATE | 3 | 4 (Architekt, Designer, Engineer, Researcher) | Komplexe Entscheidungen |

## Trigger

- "council debate"
- "council quick"
- "council expert"
- "multi-agent discussion"
- "structured debate"

## Workflow

### DEBATE (3 Rounds)
1. Runde 1: Initial Positions (parallel)
2. Runde 2: Responses & Challenges (parallel)
3. Runde 3: Final Synthesis (parallel)
4. Council Synthesis

### QUICK (1 Round)
1. 4 Agenten parallel (Advocate, Critic, Synthesizer, Expert)
2. Direkte Aggregation

### EXPERT (2 Rounds)
1. Round 1: Expert Perspectives (parallel)
2. Round 2: Synthesis durch einen Agenten
